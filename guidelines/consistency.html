Consistency

For a system to be intuitive and user-friendly, it should maintain both internal and external consistency, using consistent patterns throughout its own interface while also aligning with established conventions from the web, platform, and domain it operates in.

Internal Consistency

Internal consistency relates to consistency within a product or a family of products, either within a single application or across a family or suite of applications.

External Consistency

External consistency refers to established conventions in an industry or on the web at large, beyond one application or family of applications.
